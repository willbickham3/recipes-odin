Cereal Treats

Description

This was (and still is!) one of my most favorite desserts. My mom and grandmother would make these tasty little treats all of the time. Even today I occassionally make them for my family! These treats will have your family begging for the recipe!

Ingredients

  • 3 Cups of Corn Chex
  • 2 Cups of Frosted Flakes
  • 2 Cups of Cocoa Puffs
  • 1 Cup of Granular Sugar
  • 1 Tablespoon of Butter
  • 1 Cup of Karo Syrup
    • I prefer the karo dark syrup as it tastes a little sweeter
  • 1 1/4 Cup of Peanut Butter
  • 1 1/2 Teaspoon of Vanilla

Steps

  1. In a large mixing bowl combine the Corn Chex, Frosted Flakes and Cocoa Puffs.
  2. In a medium cooking pot mix the karo syrup, sugar and butter and heat until boiling and mixed well.
  3. Add the peanut butter and vanilla into the medium cooking pot and stir until mixed well.
  4. Once fully mixed, pour the cooking pot mixture over the cereal and mix once again.
  5. Spoon out desired size of final mixture and allow to set up on a cooking sheet or wax paper.
  6. Once cooled enough dig in and enjoy!
